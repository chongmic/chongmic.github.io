Social Media As
It Should Be

Forget the likes and unwanted posts. It's time to focus on what brings you joy.

What gives you satisfaction? Joyfill is a social media application that cultivates happiness into people's lives. It helps you evaluate and invest in people's joy, it brings genuine and intentional experiences and relationships, and it accomodates for fluctuating desires.
